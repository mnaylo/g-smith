George E. Smith

George E. Smith, 68 of Hurricane, formerly of New Hampshire, died November 12, 2014. George is a graduate of Portsmouth High School and Concord University. He was president of the TKE Fraternity and graduated from James Madison University. George is a retired school teacher from Scott Teays Elementary School.

He is preceded in death by his parents George and Ina Smith. He is survived by his wife of 45 years, Mrs. Catherine Naylor-Smith; children Matthew Smith and wife Cris Garabato-Smith, Leah Wallace and husband Brian and Whitney Naylor-Smith; six grandchildren Jana, Colin, Valan, Kherington, Jade and Ellery and many other family and friends.
